TWISTER MACHINE

A replica of the retro bubble gum twister machine, each time the user turns the button, two pills fall, one to make you feel full and one to maintain the feeling of chewing your food.

· A retro bubble gum machine with two compartments

· When the user wants to eat, they can turn the button

· Two pills will drop at once, eaten separately, to keep you full

Satiety Pill

They rise up in your stomach and keep you feeling full for five hours, so you don't feel hungry all the time

First layer: sugar coating, which melts quickly after swallowing

Layer 2: Protective rim, elastic and prevents the sponge-like structure from becoming rough on the surface as it grows

Layer 3: sponge-like balls that will slowly grow to make you feel full and then slowly dissolve

Layer 4: Nutrients, even if you don't eat, you can still meet your normal nutritional needs

Gum

Served with a satiety pill, it allows you to simulate eating all the time without worrying about over-consumption of calories

· The bubble gum is made from non-toxic resin and tastes like real food

· You can customise your bubble gum to match your favourite foods
